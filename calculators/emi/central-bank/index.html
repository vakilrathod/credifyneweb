Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Central Bank of India home loans

What is the current home loan interest rate for Central Bank of India?

Central Bank of India currently offers home loan interest rates starting from 7.35% and going up to 9.25% per annum. The exact rate depends on various factors including:

  • •Your Credit score (750+ preferred)
  • •Loan amount and loan-to-value ratio
  • •Employment type (salaried/self-employed)
  • •Property location and type

How much home loan can I get from Central Bank of India?

Central Bank of India offers home loans ranging from ₹1 lakhs to ₹5 crores. Your eligibility depends on:

Eligibility Criteria:

Salaried Individuals:

  • • Age: 21-65 years
  • • Min income: ₹25,000/month
  • • Work experience: 2+ years

Self-Employed:

  • • Age: 25-70 years
  • • ITR: 3+ years
  • • Business stability: 5+ years

What is the processing fee for Central Bank of India home loan?

Central Bank of India charges a processing fee of 0.5% of the loan amount:

Processing Fee:0.5% + GST
Minimum:₹2,000
Maximum:₹20,000

Note: This is a one-time non-refundable fee. Some banks offer processing fee waivers during festive seasons.

Can I prepay my Central Bank of India home loan?

Yes, Central Bank of India allows both partial and full prepayment of home loans:

Floating Rate Loans:

No prepayment charges as per RBI guidelines

Fixed Rate Loans:

May attract 2-4% prepayment penalty

Pro Tip: Making even small prepayments can save significant interest. A ₹50,000 annual prepayment on a ₹50L loan can save ~₹15L in interest!
