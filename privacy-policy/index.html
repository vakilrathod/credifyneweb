Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Credifyy's privacy policy is governed by one fundamental principle - maintain your trust and confidence when handling your personal information in strict compliance with regulations including the Information Technology Act 2000, Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules 2011, and the Reserve Bank of India's guidelines on data protection.

You will always be able to choose what personal information you provide to us and how such information is used - all our forms/registrations will have an explicit opt-out clause as required under data protection regulations. However, please note we may not be able to provide you with our services if you do not provide us with the requested information. We will protect your personal information by maintaining physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards as per law, including implementing the SOC2, ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standards as recognized by authorities.

We will train our employees in the proper handling of personal information. When we use other companies to provide services, we require them to protect the confidentiality of the personal information we receive from you. Our privacy policy describes how we handle your information when you visit our website, use our mobile application, and receive emails / SMS from Credifyy.

Credifyy is committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and security of all its users' information. This Privacy Policy describes how Credifyy collects, stores, handles, and transfers certain information received from you via the use of the Website or a Mobile App on a need basis. This Privacy Policy applies to our Website or Mobile App visitors and our existing and future customers. By visiting and/or using our Website, you accept and consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

This policy does not cover information you may provide to third-party websites we advertise or social media sites where we have a profile. The privacy policies on those sites will govern these. We recommend reviewing their policies if you access such services through our links.

By using or continuing to use the Credifyy websites, you agree to our use of your information (including sensitive personal information as defined under the applicable laws including, but not limited to, Information Technology Act 2000, Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules 2011, applicable rules, notification, etc.) in accordance with this Privacy Policy, as may be amended from time to time by Credifyy at its sole discretion. This consent is in compliance with the requirements of data protection laws.
